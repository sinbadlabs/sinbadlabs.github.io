What is ColorPad?

ColorPad is a coloring app for children and families. It gives kids a simple place to choose a page, pick gentle colors, and keep creating without the app getting in the way.

The experience is built for a slower kind of screen time: focused, cozy, and easy for kids to understand.

Features

  • Made for creative flow

    Kids spend more time coloring and less time figuring out what to do next.

  • Gentle color choices

    Warm, approachable palettes help each page feel inviting without overwhelming young creators.

  • Simple tools

    Clear controls keep the focus on creating, not adjusting settings.

  • A gallery that feels close

    Finished creations stay nearby in a private local gallery kids can return to.

  • Quieter screen time

    Designed for calm creative moments instead of fast rewards or noisy loops.

  • Comfortable touch interaction

    Made for family devices, with an experience that feels natural on iPad and iPhone.

For Families

ColorPad is not built as high-stimulation entertainment. It is meant to support quieter, more creative screen time that parents and kids can share at their own pace.

The experience favors clear choices, gentle visuals, and fewer moments where software interrupts the act of creating.
