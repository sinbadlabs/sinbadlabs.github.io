ColorPad

A calm coloring app for kids and families.

Simple, cozy, and designed for unhurried creative time.

What is ColorPad?

ColorPad is a coloring app for children and families. It offers a simple place to choose a coloring page, pick gentle colors, and spend quiet creative time together.

The app is built for a slower kind of screen time: focused, cozy, and easy for kids to understand.

Features

  • Kid-friendly coloring pages

    Simple artwork made for relaxed coloring sessions.

  • Cozy color palettes

    Warm, approachable palettes that help each page feel inviting.

  • Simple brushes and tools

    Clear controls that keep the focus on coloring, not settings.

  • Local gallery

    Finished creations can stay close by in a private local gallery.

  • Calm creative time

    Designed for quiet moments instead of fast rewards or noisy loops.

  • Designed for iPad and iPhone

    Made for family devices and comfortable touch interaction.

For Families

ColorPad is not built as high-stimulation entertainment. It is meant to support quieter, more creative screen time that parents and kids can share at their own pace.

The experience favors clear choices, gentle visuals, and a sense of ownership over each finished picture.
